Personal Statement

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m all about finding the sweet-spot where high-end tech meets stuff people actually enjoy using. Whether I’m creating AI-integrated web apps or building games you control with a wave of your hand, I’m obsessed with making new complex technology easy and accessible. This site is a look at my favorite projects, the ones that kept me up all night and the ones I’m excited to show off. Thanks for checking out what I’ve been working on.

Artifacts

AI-Integrated Web App

Reading should be an adventure, so I built a web app that helps kids create their own book clubs. I integrated AI to spark discussion and connect kids with stories, focusing heavily on intuitive UX design.

Motion-Controlled Game

An endless runner built in Unity using LEAP motion. Features a unique sabotage mechanic where a second player uses Rock-Paper-Scissors gestures to thwart progress in real-time.

The Bardo Experiment

An interactive comic exploring the nonlinear experience of grief. Blends minimalist design and platformer interactivity to mirror emotional processing through scientific metaphors.
