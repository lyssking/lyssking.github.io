Personal Statement

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m all about the spot where high-end tech meets stuff people actually enjoy using. . Whether I’m cooking up AI-integrated web apps or building games you control with a wave of your hand, I’m obsessed with making new complex technology easy and accessible. This site is a look at my favorite projects, the ones that kept me up all night and the ones I’m excited to show off. Thanks for checking out what I’ve been working on.

Artifacts

AI-Integrated Web App

Reading should be an adventure, so I built a web app that helps kids create their own book clubs. The coolest part? I integrated AI to help kids spark discussion and connect with the stories they’re reading. It was a huge deep-dive into AI integration and user experience, but seeing it come together into a tool that actually encourages kids to read and talk to each other was the best part.

Motion-Controlled Game

This project was super challenging as we had a very short amount of time to accomplish learning how to utilize and integrate AR into a Unity project. My group decided to go with the LEAP motion controller and made a fun endless runner. We also ended up making a 2 player version where the controller will recognize two different players and the opponent can stop the runner by using rock-paper-scissors to thwart player one's progress. 

TheBardo Experiment

The Bardo Experiment is an interactive comic exploring the nonlinear experience of grief through the story of Newt, a particle navigating a surreal simulation. Blending minimalist design, symbolic color palettes, animation, and platformer-style interactivity, the project uses visual storytelling and scientific metaphors to mirror the unpredictable progression of emotional processing.
